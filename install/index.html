How to install Ghostly

Your download will begin automatically. If it didn't start, click here to download.

Apple Silicon only · M1 or later · macOS 12+

01
Ghostly.dmg
Ghostly.app
Applications

Open the Ghostly installer from Downloads

Double-click Ghostly.dmg in your Downloads folder to open the installer window.

02
Ghostly.app
Applications

Drag Ghostly into Applications

Drag the Ghostly icon onto the Applications folder shortcut in the same window.

03

Open Ghostly from Applications

Launch Ghostly and grant microphone and accessibility permissions when prompted. It lives in the menu bar.

After you open Ghostly

  • · Pick a push-to-talk shortcut in Settings (the fn key works great).
  • · First launch downloads a Whisper model (500 MB–1.6 GB). After that, dictation works offline.
  • · Press your shortcut anywhere, talk, release — your words paste into the active app.
