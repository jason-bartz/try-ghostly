Names, numbers, nuance

Gets names right.
Every time.

The prospect's name is spelled "Niamh," not "Neve." Your personal dictionary learns it the first time — and the next 40 emails, RFPs, and CRM notes get it right.

Ghostly tags, unit-corrects, and formats automatically: ARR, MRR, seat counts, dollar values, and renewal dates come through cleanly.

"tell niamh we close tuesday"
→ "Tell Niamh we close Tuesday." ✓ spelled correctly
"arr goes from eighty four k to two twenty"
→ "ARR goes from $84K to $220K." ✓ unit-formatted
"renewal may thirty, send proposal thursday"
→ "Renewal: May 30. Send proposal: Thursday." ✓ date-tagged
"loop in sahaj and reid on next steps"
→ "Loop in Sahaj and Reid on next steps." ✓ names learned
